Key Operational Policies

System Availability Window

The system will be enabled for updates starting 4 days before until 1 day after the assigned Monday of each bi-weekly update week.

Microsoft Teams Notifications

Automated alerts are sent via Microsoft Teams. Notifications begin 4 days prior to the deadline and continue until the day of the update.

Automatic Compliance Enforcement

Once the deadline passes, the application will automatically register any unsubmitted information. These entries will be marked with a 0% compliance status.
